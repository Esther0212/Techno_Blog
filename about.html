Hi, I Am Esther Beuthel Eblacas!

I’m a BS in Information Technology student at the University of Science and Technology of Southern Philippines. I and my group are currently developing "Adoptable," an innovative app aimed at simplifying the process of adopting cats and dogs. I'm eager to engage with individuals and organizations in the pet adoption community to gather insights and feedback, ensuring that our solution effectively meets their needs and enhances the adoption experience for both pets and families.

Meet the group!

We, the group Di Na Saur, consisting of Marisol Datahan, Ainez Anquillano, Kyla Gabriento, and Ezra Mariñas, are excited to propose our innovative system, Adoptable. Our mission is to simplify and enhance the pet adoption process for both prospective pet owners and animal shelters. We envision a world where every cat and dog finds a loving home, and our app facilitates connections between these animals and families, ensuring a seamless and joyful adoption experience. Our tagline, “Bringing paws and people together,” embodies our commitment to fostering meaningful relationships between pets and their future families.

In conclusion, we believe that Adoptable will not only transform the way people adopt pets but also promote responsible pet ownership and community engagement. We are committed to making a positive impact in the pet adoption sector and are eager to gather insights and feedback from the community to refine our solution further. Together, we can make a difference in the lives of pets and their future families, fulfilling our vision of a compassionate and connected community.
